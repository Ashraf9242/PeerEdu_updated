## **Key Pages You Can Edit:**

- **`app/page.tsx`** - Main landing page with hero, features, mission/vision
- **`app/login/page.tsx`** - Login form page
- **`app/register/page.tsx`** - Registration form page
- **`app/about/page.tsx`** - About us page with company details
- **`contexts/language-context.tsx`** - All translations (English/Arabic)


## **Main Components:**

- **`components/navigation.tsx`** - Header with logo, menu, theme toggle
- **`components/footer.tsx`** - Footer with contact info & social links
- **`components/theme-toggle.tsx`** - Dark/light mode switcher
- **`components/language-switcher.tsx`** - English/Arabic language toggle
